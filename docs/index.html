DeerTracker

DeerTracker is a feature-rich app that uses state of the art object detection to organize your trail cam photos and creates profiles of the animals you want to track.

Coming soon to Google Play

Features

  • Use device GPS to save trail cam locations to a map
  • Quickly load SD card while in the field
    • GPS data automatically attaches to photos
  • View SD card history
    • Filter by time range and location
    • Manage photos
  • Object Detection
    • Detects animals, people, and vehicles with 99% accuracy
    • Detects different types of animal to over 90% accuracy
    • Distinguishes between buck and doe
    • Automatically discards empty photos
  • View sighting history
    • Sightings are highlighted within the photos
    • Filter by time range, object type, and location
    • Manage photos
  • Share photos with other apps and people
  • Profiles are automatically created for detected objects
    • View sighting predictions, maps, and statistical charts
  • Review new sightings and create custom profiles
    • Predictions include week day, time, moon phase and location
    • Charts with breakdown of sightings per week day, time, moon phase and location
    • Map shows density of sightings
